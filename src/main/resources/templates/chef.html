Chef Daniel

Chef Rochelle Daniel discovered her passion for cooking at the age of 15 and soon embarked on an illustrious culinary journey that has led her to her current role as head Chef of the award-winning Cress on Oak Creek and Etch Kitchen. Her culinary expertise spans multiple cuisines, and she is known for her innovative approach to traditional dishes. Chef Daniel has received numerous accolades and awards throughout her career, and she continues to inspire aspiring chefs around the world.
